Ford GT


The Ford GT is Ford's best attempt at a supercar.
It is a race car for the road, and it will be faster around a racetrack than pretty much anything.

  • Top Speed - 347 km/h
  • 0 -> 100km/h - 3.0s
  • Horsepower - 660 bhp
  • Torque - 746 NM
  • Engine - 3.5L V6
  • Weight - 1385 kg
  • Drivetrain - RWD
  • Price - $500,000
