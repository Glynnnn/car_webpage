Ferrari Laferrari


The Ferrari Laferrari has become one of the most iconic Ferrari's of all time.
The Laferrari is a hybrid, powered by both an electric motor and a combustion engine.
It is street legal and bound to turn the heads of anyone in the vicinty.

  • Top Speed - 349 km/h
  • 0 -> 100km/h - 2.5s
  • Horsepower - 949 bhp
  • Torque - 899 NM
  • Engine - 6.3L V12 + electric motor
  • Weight - 1255 kg
  • Drivetrain - RWD
  • Price - $1,420,000
