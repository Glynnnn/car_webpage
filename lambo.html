Lamborghini Adventador


The Lamborghini Adventador is one of the most menacing and awesome looking cars ever made.
The angles and flamboyancy of the Adventador are like nothing else.
No wonder it is one of the most popular supercars.

  • Top Speed - 350 km/h
  • 0 -> 100km/h - 2.8s
  • Horsepower - 759 bhp
  • Torque - 720 NM
  • Engine - 6.5L V12
  • Weight - 1525 kg
  • Drivetrain - 4WD
  • Price - $789,000
